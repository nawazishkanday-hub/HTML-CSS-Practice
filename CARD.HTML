Card

card is a container that holds information about a single subject. It is often used in web design to display content in a visually appealing way. A card typically includes an image, a title, and a description. It can also include additional elements such as buttons or links. Cards are commonly used in social media platforms, e-commerce websites, and news websites to showcase products, articles, or user profiles. They provide a concise and organized way to present information to users.
